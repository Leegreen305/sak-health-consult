Meet the Founder

Your Health, Our Mission

Dr. Silas Anan-Kakabaah

Medical Doctor & Telemedicine Physician

Dr. Silas Anan-Kakabaah is a medical doctor and telemedicine physician with experience in community, preventive, and occupational health. He works with patients in mining and industrial settings, managing both acute and chronic conditions while addressing workplace-related health risks.

He is the founder of SAK Health Consult, a service that brings medical care to homes, workplaces, and through telemedicine. Currently practicing at Euracare Mine Hospital, he leads clinical meetings for doctors, contributing to improved standards of care.
