No Return Policy

All items purchased through SAK Health Consult — including medical consumables, diagnostic equipment, instruments, PPE and supplies — are non-returnable and non-refundable once purchased. Part Payment Policy for Bulk Orders

For bulk and institutional purchases, SAK Health Consult offers a structured part payment arrangement to ease procurement. The following terms apply:

  • A minimum deposit of 50% of the total order value is required to confirm and process a bulk order.
  • The remaining balance must be settled in full prior to delivery or collection of goods.
  • Payment plans may be negotiated for standing institutional orders — contact our sales team to discuss terms.
  • SAK Health Consult reserves the right to withhold delivery until full payment or agreed instalment has been received.
  • All part payment agreements must be confirmed in writing via email or official purchase order.
