Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy during every healthcare interaction.

Information We Collect

We collect contact details, appointment preferences and any medical information necessary to provide safe and effective care. We only gather information that supports your service delivery and communication with our team.

How We Use Information

Your information is used to schedule visits, coordinate logistics, send appointment reminders and optimise the services you receive. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Security and Storage

We maintain secure systems and access controls to protect your data. Sensitive information is stored safely and only shared with authorised care team members as needed.

Your Rights

You can request access to your information, corrections, or removal when it is no longer needed. Contact us at info@sakhealthconsult.com for any privacy-related inquiries.
