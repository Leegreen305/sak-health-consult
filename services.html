Doctor Door (Dr. D'Or) — Home Healthcare Visits

Our signature Doctor Door (Dr. D'Or) service — meaning 'Doctor at your door' — brings qualified healthcare professionals directly to your home. Whether you need routine check-ups, nursing care, medication administration, or post-operative monitoring, our team comes to you.

Available 24/7, our home healthcare team provides compassionate, expert care tailored to your needs — in the comfort and privacy of your own space.

What's Included

  • Doctor at your doorstep consultations
  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Wound care
  • Medication administration
  • Chronic disease management
  • Post-operative care
  • Elderly care
