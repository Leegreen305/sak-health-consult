About SAK Health Consult

Bridging the gap between quality healthcare and the communities that need it most — right here in Tarkwa and across Ghana's Western Region.

Our Story

Who We Are

SAK Health Consult was founded in Tarkwa to address the critical gaps in healthcare accessibility across Ghana's Western Region. We recognised that quality medical care should not be confined to hospital walls — it should reach people wherever they are, in their homes, workplaces and communities.

Today, we deliver a comprehensive range of services including home healthcare visits, medical logistics and supply, health education programmes and community health screenings. Whether serving individuals managing chronic conditions, organisations seeking corporate wellness solutions, or communities in need of preventive care — we are there.

Our vision is a future where quality healthcare is within reach for every person in Ghana's Western Region and beyond. We are committed to building healthier communities, one home, one family and one neighbourhood at a time.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, compassionate and professional healthcare services directly to individuals and communities — removing barriers, restoring dignity and improving health outcomes across Ghana's Western Region.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Accessibility

We believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege. We remove geographic, financial and social barriers so that every person can access the care they deserve.

Quality

We uphold the highest standards in every service we deliver — from clinical care and medical supplies to education programmes and community screenings.

Compassion

Empathy is at the heart of everything we do. We treat every patient, family and community with dignity, kindness and genuine care.

Education

We empower individuals and communities through knowledge — equipping them with the information and skills to make informed health decisions and lead healthier lives.
