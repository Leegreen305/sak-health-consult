About SAK Health Consult

We are a premium healthcare provider creating trusted, convenient and community-centered medical support across Tarkwa and beyond.

WHO WE ARE

A local healthcare partner with an enterprise standard of care.

SAK Health Consult blends clinical skill, operational reliability and personalised service to deliver a healthcare experience that feels thoughtful at every touchpoint.

From urgent support to preventative education, our team is committed to making healthcare accessible, dignified and dependable for families and organisations across the Western Region.

Mission

To bring compassionate, high-quality medical care into homes and communities through trusted logistics, education and screening services.

Vision

To be the leading healthcare partner in the region, known for convenience, clarity and meaningful impact on community wellness.

OUR VALUES

Principles that guide every visit and every partnership.

Compassion

Excellence

High standards in clinical practice, logistics and community education.

Reliability

Consistent support whenever and wherever patients need care.

Connection
